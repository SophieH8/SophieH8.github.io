ISP Description

Our ISP project this year is creating an interactive webpage focused around both mental health and improving IQ. We will accomplish this through the use of problem-solving/thinking games, a chatbot feature centered around theraputal communication, and healthy food recipes that are proven to help brain functionality. Since the project features many different components, we will most likely have different groups tackle different elements of our interactive website. This will be an advantage because there is more flexibility for our teammates, and we will be able to tackle our time constraint with ease using this multi-headed approach. For example, if someone comes up with a good idea, we can make it an addition to our project. Because of this, our project is very flexible and we aren't tied down to any single idea. Our seperate groups will be focused on building the IQ enhancing games, creating a working chatbot that focuses on the mental health side of our webstie, and a team creating and designing the actual website. Revenue will be obtained from advertisements on our website, however, at the same time we must make sure that the ads don't ruin our websites experience. This website will not only address the pproblem but will also provide an adequte solution.
